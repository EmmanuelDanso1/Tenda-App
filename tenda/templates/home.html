TENDA boosts performance and keeps you fulfilled by helping you manage and track your important to-dos.

Manage, Track & Complete your Daily Activities.

Key Features

Create Tasks

Create customizable tasks, with detailed descriptions.

Update Tasks

Ability to update task name and description on the fly, in case you change your mind.

Mark Completed

You can mark finished tasks as completed, so you can focus on what's left.

About

Tenda was borne out of a desire to create a tool that could help keep track of daily tasks, and also boost productivity. Tenda is from the Swahili word Kutenda which means to do. Africans are known for their dedication and work ethic and Tenda was designed with those qualities in mind, it is a tool designed to help you keep track of your daily tasks.
