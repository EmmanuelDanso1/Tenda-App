TENDA boosts performance and keeps you fulfilled by helping you manage and track your important to-dos.

Manage, Track & Complete your Daily Activities.


Key Features

Create Tasks

Create customizable tasks, with detailed descriptions.

Update Tasks

Ability to update task name and description on the fly, in case you change your mind.

Mark Completed

You can mark finished tasks as completed, so you can focus on what's left.

About

Tenda was born out of a desire to create a tool that could help keep track of daily tasks and boost productivity. The name "Tenda" comes from the Swahili word kutenda which means "to do." Inspired by the dedications and strong work ethic often assocaited with African culture, Tenda is designed to help users efficiently manage their daily tasks..
